## Yehonatan Fridman

### About
I am a Researcher and Algorithms Developer at the Israel Atomic Energy Commission since 2019 and a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at Ben-Gurion University since 2023.

My Ph.D. research focuses on recoverable algorithms for high-performance computing systems, especially with emerging memory technologies such as persistent memory (PMEM) and CXL. My broader interests include parallel and distributed computing, algorithms, artificial intelligence, computer vision, and image processing. For the fun of it, I also enjoy combinatorics and probability.

Spinning is my favorite exercise!
